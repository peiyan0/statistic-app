How to Use This Tool

Descriptive Statistics

  • Enter comma-separated numbers or select a sample dataset
  • View comprehensive statistics including:
    • Mean, median, and mode
    • Range, variance, and standard deviation
    • Quartiles and outlier detection

Linear Regression

  • Enter paired X and Y values as comma-separated lists
  • Results include:
    • Regression equation (y = mx + b)
    • RÂ² (coefficient of determination)
    • Correlation coefficient

ANOVA

  • Enter data in format: "group1:1,2,3;group2:4,5,6"
  • Detailed ANOVA table includes:
    • F-test statistic
    • Degrees of freedom
    • Sum of squares (between/within)

Confidence Intervals

  • Three types available:
    • Two Population CI: Compare means from two samples
    • Dependent Data T-Test: Analyze before/after measurements
    • Two Sample Proportion: Compare proportions between groups

Tips & Notes

  • Sample datasets are provided for quick testing
  • All calculations are performed server-side
  • Results are rounded to 4 decimal places
  • Your calculation history is saved for reference

For detailed documentation, please contact our support team.

Happy analyzing! We hope this tool helps with your statistical needs.
